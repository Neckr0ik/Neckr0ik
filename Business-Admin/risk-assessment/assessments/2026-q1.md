# 2026-Q1 Asset Risk Assessment

## Executive Summary
Assessment of risks associated with remote work and cloud scaling.

## Risk Register
| Asset | Threat | Vulnerability | Impact | Probability | Score |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| Customer PII | Data Breach | Improper ACLs | High | Medium | 12 |
| Source Code | Leak | Unprotected Repo | High | Low | 4 |
| Employee Laptops | Theft | No Encryption | Medium | Medium | 9 |

## Mitigation Plan
- Implement CSPM for cloud ACLs.
- Enforce full-disk encryption on all portable devices.
