# Security Culture & Awareness Training

Strategies for moving beyond compliance to a true security-first mindset.

## Behavioral Change Model

Using the **Fogg Behavior Model** (Motivation, Ability, Prompt) to design security habits.

## Training Modules

1.  **Gamified Phishing**: Points for reporting, no punishment for clicking (learning first).
2.  **Lunch & Learns**: Short 15-min sessions on practical security for home and work.
3.  **Security Champions**: Identifying and empowering local experts in each department.
